Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our physiotherapy services

How long does physiotherapy typically take?

Recovery time varies depending on your condition. Most patients see noticeable improvement within 2-4 weeks of consistent therapy. However, full recovery may take several weeks to months. We'll provide a realistic timeline during your initial assessment based on your specific situation.

Do you treat stroke and paralysis patients?

Yes, we specialize in stroke and paralysis recovery. Our specialized rehabilitation programs focus on restoring movement, strength, and independence. We've successfully helped numerous stroke patients regain their mobility and quality of life.

Is home-based physiotherapy available?

Yes, we offer home therapy services for patients who are unable to visit our clinic. Our therapists will come to your home with portable equipment to provide the same quality of care in a comfortable environment.

How often should I attend therapy sessions?

Treatment frequency depends on your condition and recovery goals. Most patients benefit from 2-3 sessions per week initially. However, we customize the schedule based on your needs and may adjust it as you progress. We'll discuss the best frequency for your situation during consultation.

When will I see results from therapy?

Most patients notice improvements within the first 2-3 weeks. This might include reduced pain, better movement, or increased strength. We track your progress regularly and adjust your treatment plan accordingly to ensure continuous improvement.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring any medical reports, X-rays, or imaging scans related to your condition. Also bring a valid ID and wear comfortable clothing. If you have a list of medications you're taking, that would be helpful too. Our team will ask detailed questions about your medical history.
